Mac and Cheese

Description

This dish is the OG comfort food: cheesy, delicious, full of carbs and not even a little bit healthy. It has a short list of ingredients and is easy to make. Enjoy!

Ingredients

  • 400g macaroni
  • 400ml milk
  • 150g shredded cheddar
  • 50g shredded parmesan
  • 50g butter
  • 50g flour
  • half tsp nutmeg
  • salt
  • pepper
  • butter for greasing

Steps

  1. Cook the pasta for 5 minutes in salted water, rinse and set aside.
  2. Preheat oven to 180Â°C.
  3. Melt butter in a pot. Add flour and stir until it has dissolved completely. Then add milk while stirring and season generously with salt, pepper and nutmeg.
  4. Mix cheese in a big bowl and add two thirds of the cheese to the sauce. Stir until the cheese has melted.
  5. Add the precooked pasta to the cheese sauce and mix. Pour mixture into greased baking dish and spread the remaining cheese on top. Bake for 30 minutes
