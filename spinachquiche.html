Spinach Quiche

Description

This recipe combines French sophistication with cheese. What more can you really want?

Ingredients

For the dough

  • 250g flour
  • 125g butter
  • 1 egg
  • grease for the pan
  • dried pulses or rice for baking

For the filling

  • 550g leaf spinach
  • 1 clove of garlic
  • 2 shallots
  • 2tbsp butter
  • freshly ground pepper
  • nutmeg
  • 10 cherry tomatoes
  • 100g alpine cheese
  • 3 eggs
  • 75g whipping cream
  • 100g creme fraiche

Steps

  1. For the crust, knead flour, butter, egg and salt into a smooth dough, first with kneading hooks, then with your hands. Put into the fridge for one hour.
  2. Wash the spinach and leave to drain. Peel and chop garlic and shallots and steam in the butter until glassy. Season with salt, pepper and nutmeg. Clean the tomatoes, cut them into halves and grate the cheese.
  3. Preheat the oven to 200°C.
  4. Knead the dough briefly, then roll it out into a circle with 32cm diameter. Put into a greased pan and form a brim of 2cm. Pierce with a fork several times. Cover with baking paper and pulses and bake for 15-20 minutes.
  5. For the filling, mix eggs, cream and creme fraiche and season with salt, pepper and nutmeg. Spread the spinach on the crust, put tomato halves on top and pour the mixture over it. Spread the cheese on top. Bake at 180°C for 20-30 minutes.
